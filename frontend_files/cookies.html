Cookie Notice

Last updated: February 27, 2026

What Cookies We Use

This website uses a single, strictly necessary cookie. We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking cookies.

Cookie Name Purpose Type Duration Flags
session_id Maintains your authenticated session after signing in with Spotify. Without this cookie, you would need to sign in on every page. Strictly necessary (functional) 24 hours (or until logout) HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax

What This Cookie Does NOT Do

  • It does not track you across other websites.
  • It does not contain any personal information — only a random session identifier.
  • It is not accessible to JavaScript (HttpOnly flag).
  • It is not sent over unencrypted connections (Secure flag).

If You Don’t Want Cookies

If you choose not to sign in with Spotify, no cookies are set. The portfolio site and the owner’s “My Spotify” pages work without any cookies.

You can also delete cookies at any time through your browser settings, or log out to immediately clear the session cookie.
